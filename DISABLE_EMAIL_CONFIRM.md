You need to disable Email Confirmations in the Supabase Dashboard. \n\n1. Go to your Supabase Dashboard (https://supabase.com/dashboard/project/_/auth/providers).\n2. Click on 'Auth' and then 'Providers'\n3. Expand the 'Email' provider\n4. Toggle OFF 'Confirm email'\n5. Click 'Save'\n\nAfter saving this setting, your current frontend code will instantly log the user in when creating an account.
